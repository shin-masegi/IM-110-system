### 5.4 SS測定画面

SS(浮遊物質)測定画面の仕様 is, MLSS測定画面 and basically the same, but there are some differences.

- **Measurement Item**: "SS" (Suspended Solids)
- **Unit**: "mg/L"
- **Range**:
  - Low Range: 0.0 - 20.0 mg/L
  - Medium Range: 20.1 - 50.0 mg/L
  - High Range: 50.1 - 100.0 mg/L

For other common parts, refer to the previous section (MLSS Measurement Screen).
