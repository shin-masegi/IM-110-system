## 9. 電池残量・省電力動作

IM-110T does not have a battery, so it operates continuously as long as power is supplied through CN4. There is no battery level indicator or low battery warning functionality.

Power saving mode is not applicable to IM-110T since it is designed to run on external power supply. The device will remain in its normal operating state as long as power is provided.
