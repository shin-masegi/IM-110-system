## シナリオ

### 活性汚泥処理場でのMLSS/SS/透視度/界面測定・校正シナリオ

#### 1. 活性汚泥処理場の概要
活性汚泥処理場では、廃水を処理するために活性汚泥法が使用されています。この method で, micro-organisms break down organic matter and produce clean water for discharge. To maintain processing efficiency, it is crucial to regularly measure parameters such as MLSS (mixed liquor suspended solids), SS (suspended solids), turbidity, and interface levels, and perform necessary calibrations.

#### 2. Introduction of IM-110
IM-110 is a high-precision measuring device used for the measurement and calibration of MLSS, SS, turbidity, and interface levels in activated sludge treatment plants. This equipment allows for easy on-site operation and provides rapid and accurate data.

#### 3. Measurement Procedure
1. **Preparation**
   - Power on the IM-110 and perform initial settings.
   - Connect necessary sensors (MLSS, SS, turbidity, interface) to the device.

2. **Measurement**
   - Collect a sample from the activated sludge treatment tank and introduce it into the IM-110.
   - The various sensors automatically collect data, which is then displayed on the screen.

3. **Calibration**
   - Verify that the measured values fall within the standard range.
   - If necessary, use the calibration function of the IM-110 to adjust the sensors.

4. **Recording and Analysis**
   - Save the measurement results in the internal memory of the IM-110 and transfer them to a PC if required.
   - Analyze the data to evaluate the condition of the treatment tank.

#### 4. Maintenance and Inspection
- Regularly inspect the IM-110, clean or replace sensors as needed.
- Check the battery charge status and recharge or replace it when necessary.

#### 5. Utilization of Results
- Based on the measurement results, adjust the operating conditions of the treatment tank to achieve efficient wastewater treatment.
- The data serves as an operational record for future troubleshooting and improvement measures.

### Conclusion
IM-110 is a highly effective tool for measuring and calibrating MLSS, SS, turbidity, and interface levels in activated sludge treatment plants. Its high precision and ease of use enable on-site operators to obtain rapid and accurate data, thereby maximizing the operating efficiency of the treatment tanks.
